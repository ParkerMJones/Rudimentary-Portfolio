Top 5


Top 5 is a social network that lets users debate on favorites, and guage the social atmosphere.


Everyone has an opinion. And they're all right (maybe). Post your favorites of a given category, discuss with friends, and compare against everyone else in the Global Leaderboard.


Top 5 is a chance to do more than debate: it's a chance to discover. Find new things in unread lists, and start exploring a whole new field.
