Freesound Notepad


Freesound Notepad is a tool for music creators (and enthusiasts) to find interesting sounds at random.


Foraging for samples is an important part of any creative process. Sometimes we can get stuck in our methods: combing the same sources, using the same search terms, relying on paths that led us to gold in the past. But then they go stale.


Hopefully, with this tool, someone somewhere can find & create sounds that lead them in new, serendipitous directions.
